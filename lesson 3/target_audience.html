Target Audience

The target audience for our website is for those who enjoy doing outdoor activities such as running, walking, camping, etc. Our hope is that our website will help people know how to prepare for the weather so it does not interrupt their outdoor activities.

persona 1

Sabrena:Sabrena is a 21 year old college student. Something that sabrena loves to do is travel around to different places in the US. She uses the website so she can know where is safe to travel and where it might be hazerdous.

Persona 2

Steven: Steven is a 30 year old who loves to go on hikes with his family. Steven will use the website so that he can know how warm to dress while he goes on his hikes.
