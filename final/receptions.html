What we provide

Setup of the reception

Our employees will set up all chairs, tables, etc. We do require that you give us an approximate number of people so we know how many chairs and tables to set up.

food service

Our employees will make and serve the food provided. If you would like an outside source to provide food please let us know in advance.

reserved rooms

You can reserve multiple rooms so that all of your guests will be accomodated.

Transportation

If any of your guests need to be picked up our employees will drive shuttles to pick them up.

Location

Each of our hotels is surrounded by an aesthetic view that allows you to enjoy the beauty of the world around you on your special day.

Budget

Unlike a lot of hotels our services are sold at a cheap price.

Welcome Bag Distribution

If you have welcome bags that you would like to give your guests, our employees will hand them out at the door.
